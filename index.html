Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import the assets? +
Open your Unity project, navigate to Window > Package Manager, select "My Assets" from the dropdown, find your purchase, and click Download then Import.
Which Unity versions are supported? +
Most of our tools support Unity 2022.3 LTS and newer (including Unity 6). Please check the specific requirements section on individual asset store product descriptions.
Can I use these in commercial games? +
Yes, absolutely! All purchases are governed under the standard Unity Asset Store EULA license, meaning you can use them in unlimited commercial or free projects.
